| Values: | What this value means to me: | Anecdote: | The connection with hospitality |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| ***Passion*** | Passion is a feeling of love for something it can be a subject or an object. You would be willing to go around the world for it and not regret it. | I am passionate about making a smile appear on someone’s face after a long day. I have been working in hospitality for about 4 years now and during this time I have always enjoyed seeing people having a good time.  | When you have a passion for hospitality you are willing to go the extra mile. This makes it so you as a hospitality business get a better result. |
| ***Openness*** | Openness is sharing, not only about your accomplishments but also about your failures.  | From the moment I was able to talk, I started connecting and making small talk with all the people around me I met during the day. In later life, this led to some very unexpected meetings which resulted in true friendships when I traveled around the US, Spain and when I stayed in London. | Openness within hospitality is an important value because openness allows the guest to truly relax en enjoy the experience. |
| ***Inspiration*** | Inspiration to me is a wonderful idea or insight that you can get from absolutely anything, for example watching a movie. | The last time I got inspired was two weeks ago at my work. I was having a wonderful conversation with one of our more frequent guests and she challenged me to invent a new cocktail for her. This got me to ask her questions about what flavors she liked and disliked. During this, I had as you could say a quick brainstorm session in my head and there at once was the inspiration I needed, she inspired me to think out of the box, I knew what type of cocktail I was going to create for her. | Inspiration is important in hospitality because it breaks boundaries and ensures we go the extra mile.   |
| ***Initiative*** | Initiative for me is taking the lead on something that you want to accomplish and show other people a path to follow and get there. | I enjoy parties, not only the partying part but also the organizing of one. The most recent example of a party that I organized is a Halloween cocktail party that I organized recently. I lived a few months in Sevilla and threw a party for all my housemates. I used my experience as a cocktail bartender.  | Initiative is an important value within hospitality because it makes sure that things get done. |
| ***Professionalism*** | Professionalism is not only a posture, but it is also a mindset. Being able to have criticism on your own actions and then grow from the mistakes that you made and keep the good parts. | I love working in teams and getting things organized professionally. At Villa Orloff, I was one of the first part-time employees and have experienced how to build a business from scratch. I have learned a lot from my colleagues and managers. They taught me to work hard, have an eye for detail and always be hospitable. I am optimistic and do not easily give up. | Recently my cocktail bartender teacher Vince Silva said about me: “Thomas has a high work ethic ensuring punctuality and professionalism at all times. Thomas would be a great asset to any organization.” |

\[As a final remark,\] I would like to say that I truly believe in the five values of the Hotelschool Maastricht: ….
