**1st and 2nd Periodic reports**  
**Management internship starting in Sept’24**

*Complete the dedicated sections in this document. For this, please use the **Tab function** from your keyboard to jump in the document from one section to the other and your **Mouse** to select the appropriate box for drop-down list.*

**Objectives:**  
During your internship, you will need to submit 2 periodic reports in Onstage. It is important for your HMSM internship supervisor to be aware of your activities, understand your successes and challenges in order to coach you and be able to assess your internship performance at the end of the period. Additionally, in these periodic reports you are asked to write about some topics that will be part of your final report. Finally, each periodic report is also the moment to explain your progress with regards to your Business Process Improvement project. Your internship supervisor will provide you with valuable feedback and challenge you with his/her feedback. 

**Instructions:**  
You will need to complete this form as per the instructions below for each paragraph. These periodic reports, when well written, save you time when writing your Final report and/or Business Process improvement report. You will quickly notice that the periodic reports are built in a way to support you to successfully complete the final products you hand in towards the end of your internship. You must therefore study the *General Assessment Form* and the *Business Process Improvement Process Student Instruction* on Moodle (Execution phase). These documents clarify the content on which your reporting will be assessed. 

Each periodic report must be uploaded in OnStage in the corresponding activity. Each periodic report must be validated and approved by your HMSM internship supervisor. In case your periodic report is evaluated as insufficient by your internship school supervisor, you will be required to submit a new version of this periodic report.

**Deadlines\*:**

- Week 6: 1st Periodic report   
- Week 13: 2nd Periodic report

*\*Note: these weeks are calculated from the starting week of your internship. Refer to your Internship timeline to review the specific dates you and your supervisor agreed on.*
