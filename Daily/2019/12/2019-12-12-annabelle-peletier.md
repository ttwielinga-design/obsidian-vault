The Hague, March 5, 2019


Dear Sir/Madam,


My name is Sabina Randebrock and I’m teaching German Language and Culture at Maerlant-Lyceum in The Hague. Also I’m the study advisor for the students at our secondary school. I have known Annabelle Peletier since the schoolyear of 2016/2017, when I started to teach her class.

Annabelle has always been an outstanding student. She is very inquisitive and ambitious. She always strived to reach the highest marks, mediocrity has never been sufficient for her. For me as a teacher it has been a pleasure to have Annabelle in my classes.

Since 2016 I saw Annabelle develop into a confident young lady, who knows, what she wants and is able to express her thoughts and viewpoints very clearly. One of her personal characteristics is her being absolutely trustworthy. She is always willing to help classmates that needed her support and care. Annabelle has strong social relationships with her fellow students and with the teachers. We all feel, that we can really rely on her.

Also Annabelle and I share a special experience together: our exchange-weeks with German students from Bremerhaven last year. Annabelle and some other students from our school stayed one week with a German student in Bremerhaven and hosted this student at home in The Hague the next week.

During this period I got to know and appreciate Annabelle’s leadership- and teamworkskills. She not only assisted me, but she also took over when necessary and she managed to cheer everybody up when things where not going easy. At the end of the project I felt that we did this together!

It won’t come as a surprise to you, that I strongly want to recommend Annabelle to your university. I am absolutely sure that she can and will accomplish much in her study and in her life and I hope you will give her a chance for further development at your institute.

In case you need more information about Annabelle, don’t hesitate to contact me.


Yours faithfully,

Sabina Randebrock

Teacher of German/Study-advisor
randebrock@maerlant-lyceum.nl
