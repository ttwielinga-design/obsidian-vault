M6: Performance Management - Marketing Workshop 6.1  
(week 6, workshop 1) – Promotion 
Communicating Customer Value 
90 minutes (content and instruction 30 minutes, discussion 30 minutes, application 30 minutes) 
Learning goals: 
At the end of this week, you should be able to: 
1. Understand the importance of integrating the different marketing communications across the 
operations, including advertising, sales promotion, personal selling, PR, direct and digital 
marketing.  
2. Define the role of the different marketing communications and the costs/benefits linked to 
each. 
3. Understand how to develop effective marketing communications, linking these to the goals 
and strategic decisions of the organization.  
4. Understand the benefits of direct and digital marketing for both buyers and sellers. 
  
Preparation: Please listen to the Video Lecture 6.1 
on Integrating Communications which is found on 
Moodle and based on Chapters 13, 14, and 16 in 
your marketing textbook 
On Moodle and Literature:

What are you expected to know at the start of the session? 
1. You can define the different marketing communications such as advertising, sales promotion, 
personal selling, PR, direct and digital marketing.  
2. You understand the different marketing communications decisions available in HOTS and the 
costs/benefits linked to each.  
3. You recognize the importance of a review system as a feedback mechanism and the 
importance of responding to reviews or complaints from your Next Step workshop. 
4. You know how to make a blog from your English classes and Simulise workshops. 
 
Prepare and bring to the session 
1.  Strategic Marketing Plan including marketing mix strategies for product, personnel, pricing, 
and distribution. 
2. Results of HOTS Year 2 and your strategies from the digital marketing workshop 5.2 for 
improving your review management and public awareness which you implemented in Year 2 
3. Please bring an example of an article or advertisement of a digital or mobile service recently 
launched by a hospitality company  
What are we going to do in this workshop? 
• 
The first 20 minutes of this workshop, we will go over any questions on the video lecture 6.1, 
concentrating on the different types of marketing communications and the need for 
integrating these to send consistent messages to the customers and also to the secondary 
audience, your employees and marketing intermediaries.  
• 
The next 25 minutes will consist of a class discussion on how companies have responded to 
the increasing trend of digital services and other new technologies to develop online 
marketing strategies, sharing the examples you have brought along.  
• 
Last week, you worked on analyzing reviews during your digital marketing session. How can 
you influence these reviews by creating a better digital marketing platform for your hotel? In 

HOTS, you are encouraged to write a blog and interact on social media for reviews, but what 
are the effects of this type of media on the public awareness index. Use this KPI to determine 
how to improve your hotel’s market share through the use of different types of media.  
• 
In the last 45 minutes, each HOTS group will enhance the social media page for their hotel as 
a digital platform. Consider the different aspects that you have learned from the other 
marketing mix elements that could be illustrated here i.e. differentiated products and 
services, restaurant specials, promotions and events, leisure club, etc. Don’t forget to make 
food use of visuals. Choose the best blog for your F&B concept, written individually in your 
English class, and upload it on the digital marketing platform in order to integrate what has 
been learned in the last two workshops regarding the importance of online marketing.  
• 
The social media page will be evaluated by the HOTS coach, as it influences the public 
awareness and social media of the hotel for Year 3. In addition, you will need to upload your 
selected blog (from the English workshop) before Thursday, when you run the HOTS 
simulation.   
• 
At the end of the week, you will be running Year 3 based on the budget plan that you have 
been developing in your MAC classes. You will be able to evaluate how effective your blog and 
digital platform have been.  
 
How is the content of this session reflected in the test, assessment or professional product?  
• 
You are able to explain the effectiveness and costs of using mass advertising for public 
awareness, for example, versus more targeted promotion for specific target groups. There is a link 
to the strategy that has been outlined in the marketing plan.
