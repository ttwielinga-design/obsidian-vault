Module 6 Marketing
Workshop 5.2 Review Analysis 
Instruction max. 30 minutes, application max. 60 minutes; total of 90 minutes. 
 
Learning goals:
1. At the end of this workshop you will understand the importance of managing 
your reviews to improve your online reputation on the different marketing 
channels. 
2. You are going to enhance your own digital marketing platform in HOTS (social 
media page, blog), so the concepts learned in this workshop will be important 
as well as input for next week, when we will be looking at promotional 
decisions.   
 
 
To be studied before the start of the session:
1. Glance through Revinate’s 2019 Global Hotel 
Reputation Benchmark Report which you can 
find on Moodle. 
2. Watch the video ‘Reputation Management’ 
from the company Revinate, watch it carefully 
because we are going to ask you questions 
about it during the workshop. 
 
 
 
On Moodle and 
Literature: - 
 
 
What are you expected to know at the start of the session?
1. You are able to identify different distribution channels and are able to 
justify your decisions whether or not to use which OTAs with respect to 
revenues generated and distribution costs. 
2. During your operational internship in M4 you will have experienced the 
importance of review sites such as TripAdvisor. 
 
 
Prepare and bring to the session
1. Strategic Marketing Plan (SMP) with the marketing mix strategies.  
2. Results of the first 6 months of HOTS Year 2, including your channel sales CVS 
report. 
3. Reflection on the distribution strategy regarding the different distribution 
channels available. 
 
What are we going to do in this workshop?
 
First 30 minutes: 
Discussion, answering questions about improving reviews and online reputation 
management using data-driven techniques & best practices. 
Next 30 minutes: 
Application to your project hotel in HOTS: 

 
 
 
 
1. Benchmark your review management practice, using the 2019 Global Hotel 
Reputation Benchmark Report: 
a. View the reviews you generate per month through each 
distribution channel you use? In the case of HOTS, direct bookings 
generate reviews. (Look at View reviews) 
b. What is the development in these ratings over time? 
c. How do these ratings affect public awareness and sales per 
channel? (link between distribution and promotion) 
d. What was your response rate per review rating?  
e. Benchmark your average rating for your hotel class. 
f. Benchmark your average response rate for your hotel class. 
2. Create an overview of your findings (use graphs) from the above benchmark. 
3. Check if you respond to reviews in the right way: how, who, when, how much, 
using the tips in the webinar ‘Reputation Management’ of Revinate. 
4. Check which actions you are able take from the below toolbox within HOTS. 
 
5. Formulate the necessary actions, based on the outcome of 1, 2, 3 and 4 to 
improve your review management and online reputation (and execute these 
actions during your next HOTS session). Take them with you to workshop 6.1 
as well. 
Final 30 minutes: 
Present the necessary actions, based on the outcome of 1, 2, 3 and 4 to improve 
your review management and online reputation to your lecturer and explain how 
these actions will contribute to the KPI ‘public awareness’ of your project hotel. 
How is the content of this session reflected in the test, assessment or 
professional product?  
• 
You are able to reflect on how your review management contributed to 
improve the online reputation of your project hotel, in line with the hotel 
concept. Take this reflection into account when you are going to build a digital 
marketing platform in workshop 6.1. 
• 
You are able to explain how your review management has contributed to the 
KPI ‘public awareness’ of your project hotel.
