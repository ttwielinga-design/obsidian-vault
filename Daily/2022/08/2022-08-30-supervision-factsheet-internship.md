Dear Internship Supervisor, 
First of all, thank you for welcoming one of our students in your department for her/his internship. This 
internship, as part of the curriculum of the Hotel Management School Maastricht, is seen as a significant 
and unique period during our education.  
At the end of this period, our students are enriched by the expertise you exposed them to and have grown 
to young professionals. Please, therefore do not hesitate to challenge them as much as you can in order to 
help them see the bigger picture.  
We aim to develop and strengthen a close relationship with you regarding the internship program. That is 
crucial to us; it will help us serve you better and help us educate our students even further. 
 
Internships & objectives 
The Hotel Management School Maastricht offers an international four-year bachelor program, aiming to 
prepare students to become tomorrow’s leader of the hospitality industry. As part of our four-year 
education, students have to pursue two mandatory internships. 
During her/his internship, the student needs to perform regular operational tasks. However, as a school 
institution, we want the student to go deeper, to be involved, and to be curious in order to understand how 
your company functions as a whole. This can be done by exposing the student to some extra tasks, by 
giving her/him some new projects, by allowing her/him to do some cross-exposure, etc. 
For each of the two internships, our school has established some general objectives as follows:

Operational Internship:

 Analyze the business model of the organization and how

 Understand and answer to guests’ needs and

 Identify and reflect on inter-cultural aspects; 
 Establish a number of learning and development goals

Management Internship: 
Fourth Year 
 
 Understand the structure and processes of the 
department and company as a whole; 
 Independently and pro-actively master a scope of 
managerial tasks; 
 Evaluate the mission and vision, leadership styles and 
communication streams; 
 Implement a Business Process Improvement for the 
company with evaluation of the first results; 
 Contribute to realizing company’s KPI’s; 
 Establish a number of learning and development goals 
to pursue during the placement. 
 
 
 
 
 
Business Process Improvement 
Project – Management 
internship 
 
Besides the daily tasks, the fourth year students doing their 
management internship must complete a business process 
improvement project. We expect the student to observe the 
departmental business processes and determine one of 
choice, at the very start of their internship, so that the 
company can benefit from the student’s investigation and 
advice. The project scope should represent about 10% of 
the duration of the internship, and we greatly appreciate if 4 
hours per week could be allocated for the student during 
working hours. 
 
Reporting & Supervision  
 
Besides the daily tasks, the student will be assigned to a 
school supervisor with whom she/he will communicate on a 
regular basis via two periodic reports. On top of these 
reports, evaluations that assess the development and 
performance of the student are expected, and this is where 
we need your support as a company supervisor. An overview 
of these moments is given in the chart below.
