# Chapter one; Justification of ingredients
When creating our dishes for gastronomic lunch, we tried to come up with healthy and seasonal products to use. The choices were made with a lot of thought, thinking about sustainable ways to receive our products. Important to us was creating a tasty and interesting dish and yet trying to keep things simple. Therefore, the preparation of the ingredients is fairly simple, but very refreshing as you will not see this very often.

For our first dish, we chose the beautiful dogfish. This is an atypical and unknown small shark type to most people, that lives in the waters around Western Europe. The fish will contribute to low transportation costs as it comes straight from the North Sea. It is mostly caught as side catch by fishermen, and therefore sustainable. We will be complimenting the dogfish with parsnip and cucumber, both vegetables locally grown in The Netherlands. Parsnip grows during winter and therefore a product that is in season right now. When looking for seasonal and sustainable products, these ingredients thick the boxes.

For our second dish, we picked turbot fish. August until February is the prime season of this fish, that contains a lot of healthy vitamins and minerals. Next to the turbot fish, on the plate will be shiitake mushrooms. These mushroom will definitely thick the box of sustainability,  as we will grow these ourselves on a bed of sawdust. As you can grow these mushrooms inside, there is no season connected and therefore always in season. This way, we wanted to make our dish locally resourced and therefore sustainable.

Looking at all the ingredients, the main theme of our gastronomic lunch are dishes made from locally grown, sustainable and seasonal products. It is important to be more conscious about the things you eat and the impact it has on the environment, which explains our choice of products.

# Chapter three; Allergens
Dogfish
In this dish, there are two allergens to be found, fish and lactose. Mild allergic reactions to eating fish or shellfish can be seen on the skin as red bumps, hives or an itchy skin. Other mild reactions are swelling of the lips, a tingling or tightening feeling in the mouth and throat, a runny nose or digestive problems such as cramps, stomach pain, nausea or vomiting. . Severe reactions lead to difficulties in breathing, swelling of the tongue and throat or tightness of the throat, coughing and dizziness. Eventually someone with a severe reaction can collapse. Young children with fish allergy get pale or tend to flop or hang. The lactose allergy comes from the heavy cream, butter and milk. Symptoms for this allergy are a bloated feeling, stomach cramps, or diarrhea.

Asian turbot
The two allergens mentioned above, fish and lactose, also apply to this dish. The third allergen in this dish is egg. Mild reactions can be hives. A severe reaction leads to anaphylaxis, the stadium before this can be recognised by the person having a rapid heartbeat, fainting, dizziness and swelling  . An allergic reaction to soy can range from hives, a tingling or itchy feeling in the mouth or of the skin. Swelling of mouth and throat area, abdominal pain, diarrhea and nauseousness and vomiting. A severe reaction will lead to an anaphylactic shock.

# Chapter five; Food and beverage calculation
HANOS was the source for our food and beverage cost calculations. When creating the dishes, we thought it was fun to make a more modest dish and one a bit more exotic with a lot of Asian influences. Because these products are available to a lesser extent, it added up in the food costs making it a bit more expensive.
Some products were not available at HANOS, to still make sure to have an article number, we used alternative shops as our source for the products. These products are marked with a star (*) in the sheet, with behind the name of the source mentioned.
Important to mention, the food is calculated with the right VAT of 9% and the wine with a VAT of 21%, according to the Dutch regulations. The food cost per person is shown in yellow. For the dogfish dish, the total amount per portion is €3,30, for the dish with turbot €9,61.
