Module 9 Leadership – Expert Workshop  
‘Coaching as a Leadership Style’ 
 
 120 minutes  
 
Do you want to experience the feeling of coaching as a leadership style? Do you 
want to know how to intrinsically motivate your employees to get things done? Do 
you want to empower your people and lead them to be independent powerful 
operators? Then join this workshop in which you will learn about “Coaching as a 
leadership style”. Coaching is a powerful method, instrument, and style which can 
create independent and motivated employees who feel seen and wanted by their 
manager. Connecting with people in a respectful and human way is an important 
foundation to having a successful team and fully matches with internal hospitality. 
 
Learning goals 
At the end of this session:  
• 
The student is able to apply the four AAAA’s in order to actively engage in a 
respectful conversation with another person in the context of 
manager-employee relationship 
• 
The student as a leader is able to set a common goal in agreement with an 
employee as part of a work agreement 
• 
The student is able to reflect on his/her style of coaching and the basis that 
aligns with creating rapport to foster a powerful connection

What are we going to do in this meeting? 
• 
After preparing the important core materials beforehand, we will work 
with a hands-on approach during the workshop. About 75% of the 
workshop will involve active practice from you with your fellow students. 
You will experience the power of asking questions, receive constructive 
feedback, and reflect on your leadership style.
