# Management Questions:

Financials & Performance

Does the property have a Balanced score Card? - No
Can you share the Balance sheet and Cash flow statement for 2022/2023? – see P&L 2022 and BUD 2023 (Shared with Ko and Rick)
The payroll expenses for "Penta Wiesbaden" in the Rooms department are lower than the industry average (10.6% vs. 15.4%). How can you maintain this lower expense, and is there a potential to increase payroll to improve the guest experience or employee satisfaction? – salaries in rooms are paid after by local collective agreement in Hesse. We are looking to hire 1,2 more people in rooms to increase the guest experience but also the team satisfaction.   We also need to remember that the Penta staffing model means we have staff in the Pentalounge that cover both FO & F&B so can be that some FO payroll is charged to F&B
From what we have calculated your food costs % is 44,1%, why do you think your food costs are so much higher than the German average of 28,4%? – please check P&L 2022 – food cost in 2022 by 32,75%
You said you’d like to invest 3 million in the lounge area and the meeting rooms. How do you plan on dividing the money over those 2 and which one would get the priority? -  priority is our lounge: we need new aircondition, heating and furniture there. If there is still money left we will invest in mice: equipment ( technology and tables )
Can you tell us how much the CAPEX is and how you use this within your hotel? Each hotel prepares a capex budget for the  following year as part of the annual budget process.  This is reviewed with owners and then priototies are agreed across the portfolio. We assume 1.5M this year which will then be added to as we go in to 2024
What is the occupancy on weekends and weekdays? Is there a big difference? – OCC on weekdays are much higher because of the business in Wiesbaden. Also PH Wiesbaden is mainly focussed on business and meeting guests. There is no spa area as well as local attraction close to the hotel why weekend occupancy is lower. Nevertheless there is still room to improve occupancy, but not for every price ( low ADR is no option as we need to take into account break even point).

Are you planning any small/ major renovations in the near/ far future that could have an impact on your capital expenditure? We will be installing air con in to the lounge and as these works are intrusive will include re-dec and plan to invest in new FFE at the same time.  We also have quite some  technical investments we need to do to the building which we are discussing with owners, eg façade, plant room
What factors does the hotel want to change to optimize profit? For this year we want to increase ADR and lower OCC for sure, but also planning with small new details, renovations and changes in Operations to increase the profit- i.e. new terrace concept
We are rather looking for more value business due to high rising costs.

Why are the marketing costs so low? Every hotel is charged 1.5% of their revenue on sales and marketing costs in addition to the cost lines you see in the P&L.   We then do ad hoc marketing as needed for certain venues.
Why have the costs for IT systems increased? – after a big hacking in 2021 penta increased IT security and needed to invest in completely new devices for entire chain.
