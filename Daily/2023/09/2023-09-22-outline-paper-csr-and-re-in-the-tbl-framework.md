INTRODUCTION
Focus Topic: CSR and RE in the TBL Framework
Explanation of how CSR and responsible entrepreneurship connect to the TBL dimensions of People, Planet, and Profit.
The significance and importance of this topic in contemporary business and research

Research Proposal
Explain why the focus is on CSR, RE, and their interplay in the context of the TBL framework.

Research Question
How do CSR and RE influence business sustainability across the dimensions of People, Planet, and Profit?
1: What is the impact of CSR on business sustainability, particularly in terms of People?
2: How does CSR affect business sustainability concerning the Planet (environmental impact)?
3: In what ways does CSR influence business sustainability in relation to Profit (financial performance)? Supporting questions:

BODY
Theme 1: CSR, RE, and Responsibility in Business
Main idea: CSR and Responsibility in Business are gaining prominence.
Supporting idea 1: Firms emphasize corporate responsibility (Tiba et al., 2018, p. 265).
Supporting idea 2: CEOs recognize the importance of non-financial success (PWC, 2016).
Supporting idea 3: Governments and NGOs advocate for increased corporate responsibility (Godfrey & Hatch, 2007).

Theme 2: CSR and Its Impact on People
Main idea: Exploring the Impact of CSR on People in Business Sustainability.
Supporting idea 1: Many firms emphasize corporate responsibility (Tiba et al., 2018, p. 265).
Supporting idea 2: CEOs recognize the importance of non-financial success (PWC, 2016).
Supporting idea 3: Governments and NGOs advocate for increased corporate responsibility (Godfrey & Hatch, 2007).

Theme 3: CSR and Its Impact on the Planet (Environmental Impact)
Main idea: Analyzing the Effects of CSR on Environmental Sustainability.
Supporting idea 1: CSR reporting lowers capital cost and enhances transparency (Hu & Zhang, 2023).
Supporting idea 2: CSR mitigates uncertainty and ethical concerns in innovation (Hu & Zhang, 2023).

Theme 4: CSR and Its Impact on Profit (Financial Performance)
Main idea: Investigating the Influence of CSR on Financial Performance.
Supporting idea 1: Startups have environmental and regulatory challenges (Fuller & Tian, 2006; Wang & Bansal, 2012).
Supporting idea 2: Examples of Uber and Airbnb facing criticism (Hofmann, 2014; Tickle, 2016).
Supporting idea 3: Investors value responsible behavior in companies (Bocken, 2015; Murguia & Lence, 2015).
