Slide 11: Step 1 - Outsourcing & Cultural Integration
1st outsource non-core activities such as HR, accounting, and legal to local companies. 2 things:
Acquire local cultural knowledge at a fast pace
Mitigate the chances of hiring unqualified professionals
2de Cultural integration
We found that: BLH must match local business culture such as formal and accurate communication & adherence to time schedules.
3de Know the language
Ensure everyone on the Exec team knows German as this is still the most important language in Germany and there is a high preference on speaking it.

Slide 12: Step 1 - Implementation Plan
When looking to implement this BLH should
Identify the local outsourcing companies and partner with them
Hire an Executive to oversee Germany for BLH
Minimum 15 years of experience
Corporate finance background
Minimum of 10 years of experience within the German hotel market
To make sure BLH’s current team is Up-To-Date on the German market hold internal seminar on German Consumer behavior.
