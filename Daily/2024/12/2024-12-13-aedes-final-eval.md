Obiective: During the internship, a student has two official moments to reflect on her/his performance and get feedback from
the company mentor. The objective of these evaluations is to know where the student stands and what she/he should focus
on in the remaining months of the internship and the development afterwards.
Timins: The student will carry out a mid-term evaluation at around the midway point of the internship (before the HMSM
internship supervisor visit or video call, and this may be well before the exact middle!). The 2nd evaluation should take place
towards the end of the internship.
Procedure:
For each evaluation (mid-term and end-term evaluation), the student should proceed as follows:
. 
Step 1: The students takes the initiative and schedules the meetings with the company mentor and him/herself and
the one with the company mentor, him/herself and the internship school supervisor
. 
Step 2: The student completes this evaluation form (self-evaluation)
. 
Step 3: The student asks her/his company mentor to complete this evaluation form (please hand-in a blank copy of
this document for the mid-term evaluation)
. 
Step 4: The student and company mentor meet to review both evaluation forms (self-evaluation and evaluation
completed by the company mentor), compare differences and discuss the student's performance
. 
Step 5: The student uploads both evaluation forms (self-evaluation and a scanned copv of companv mentor evaluation
with the companv mentor signature and companv stamp) in OnStage in the corresponding activity (mid-term or final
evaluation)
Note: for the final evaluation, the same process as above will apply. The only difference is that the student will take the mid-
term self-evaluation form and complete it with her/his final self-evaluation. The company mentor will take the mid-term
evaluation and complete with his/her final evaluation. Like this, we can see the progresses made.
