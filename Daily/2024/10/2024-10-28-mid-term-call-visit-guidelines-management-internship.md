Mid term call/visit 
Management internship  
 
Use these guidelines for your midterm visit/call with your school supervisor  
 
Objective: This call / visit is meant to evaluate your performance and learning curve over the first half of your internship and 
to agree on a way forward together with your HMSM school supervisor and your company mentor.  
 
The call/visit should take place between your 8th and 12th week after your internship starting date. Personal deadlines have 
been set down in your PDP document. 
 
Below you can find the guidelines for this call. You are expected to take the initiative and set up the necessary appointments 
with your company mentor and school internship supervisor.  
 
Important notes!  
▪ 
Before the call/ visit takes place you invite your company mentor for an evaluation meeting an pro-actively share 
the evaluation form with your company mentor. You ask him/her to fill it out and sign it at the bottom. You need to 
do the same and evaluate your own performance using a blank evaluation form (self-evaluation). During your 
evaluation meeting with your company mentor you will discuss your performance by means of discussing the 
evaluation forms that you have both filled out individually. At the end of the meeting you will agree on how to move 
forward. After your meeting you will upload the signed self- and company evaluation forms in Onstage. 
 
▪ 
During the call/ visit your HMSM school supervisor will first take time to talk to you individually where after the 
company mentor is invited to join the conversation. In the event there is a specific topic that should be addressed in 
the conversation when the company mentor has joined the conversation, please inform your HMSM supervisor 
during your one-to-one.  
 
▪ 
After the call/visit please record basic information about date, time and what was discussed in Onstage. Besides 
that also record the agreements made.  
 
 
List of questions 
Below you will find a list of possible questions that could be asked by your supervisor during the visit/call before the company 
mentor is invited to join the conversation.  
 
Internship journey so far 
Some examples of questions that could be asked 
 
a. 
How do you feel? How do you feel in this new environment? And how do you feel within the workplace? 
b.  
How do you perceive the first half of your internship? 
c.  
How do you manage to find a balance between private, work and school life?  
c.  
Did you notice any differences in terms of expectations in regards to the internship scope /responsibilities?  
i. 
If so, what differences do you experience?  
ii. 
How do you feel about this?  
iii.  
How will you tackle this? 
d. 
How are your learning goals progressing?  
e.  
What are three things that you really enjoy right now? 
f. 
What challenges are your currently facing? 
g.  
How do you look forward to the second half of your internship?

Midterm evaluation form 
Some examples of questions that could be asked 
 
a. 
What were the three most important points that were discussed in the meeting with your company mentor about 
your midterm evaluation? 
b.  
Which competences did score well? Which competencies did score relatively low?  
c. 
Were there any big differences in how you and the company mentor rated your performance? If so, which ones? 
d. 
What have you learned from the evaluation meeting with your company mentor? 
e. 
What competencies do you need to focus on in the remainder of your internship?  
f.  
What actions will you take to support growth on those competencies? 
g.  
What support do you need for that? 
 
 
Progress on reporting (incl. Business Process Improvement report) 
Some examples of questions that could be asked 
 
a. 
What developments do you see in the company right now? 
b. 
What is the impact of that on the department? (think about Business model / Leadership / …) 
c.  
How do you manage to get all (management) information necessary for completion of the report(s)? 
d. 
How is your Business Process Improvement progressing? (Step 1 to 7 should be addressed by now) 
e. 
What do you consider to be difficult for you in this process? 
f.  
What are your options to make it easier for yourself? What else can you think of?   
g.  
What challenges have you faced or do you still foresee? 
h.  
How can you stand up to these challenges and/or complete the actions/steps that are left to take? 
 
 
Plan for the remaining period 
Some examples of questions that could be asked 
 
a. 
What is on your training plan for the remaining weeks? 
b.  
What could you do (or do more of) to optimize your internship experience?  
c. 
Summarizing: what action will you take to support further personal and professional growth? 
d.  
What support do you need? From your company mentor? From the HMSM supervisor? 
 
 
Other topics  
Some examples of questions that could be asked 
 
a.  
What other feedback have you received from the company?  
b. 
How are you preparing for graduation?  
c.  
What are your thoughts about life after graduation? 
d.  
Is there anything else on your mind right now that you would like to discuss?
