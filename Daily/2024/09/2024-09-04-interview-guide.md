Interview Guide / Questions Per stakeholder

General Questions:
What lessons have been learned from the Amstel 111 project so far (and from DD looking back)?
How can these lessons be applied to future projects?
What were the biggest challenges encountered during the project, and how were they overcome?
Are there any processes or practices you would change in future projects based on your experience with Amstel 111?
Development
Project Planning and Design
What were the key objectives for the Amstel 111 project during the planning phase?
How was the initial project scope defined?
Were there any significant changes during the project?
What were the main challenges faced during the design phase, and how were they addressed?
How does the development team coordinate with architects and designers?
What’s the process for approving design changes?
Budget and Financials:
Were there any unexpected costs?
If so, what were the causes?
How does the team monitor and manage the budget throughout the project?
What financial controls are in place to prevent cost overruns?
Timeline and Milestones:
What were the original project timelines and milestones?
Do you have that in an excel sheet / flowchart for me?
Were there any delays, and what were the primary reasons for these delays?
How does the development team ensure that the project stays on schedule?
What is the process for updating stakeholders on timeline changes?
Stakeholder Engagement
How does the development team interact with other stakeholders, such as project management, hospitality, and asset management?
What communication methods are used to ensure all parties are aligned?
Were there any communication difficulties?
If so, how were they resolved?

Project Management
Project Execution
Can you walk me through the project management process used for Amstel 111?
How do you make sure all stakeholders are on the same page?
How are project risks identified, monitored, and mitigated throughout the project?
Resource Allocation
How are resources (both time/human and material) allocated across different phases of the project?
Were there any resource constraints that impacted the project?
How were they handled?
How does is the workload balanced among different teams and departments?
Quality Control
What quality control measures were implemented to ensure project deliverables met the required standards?
How are these being tracked and documented (quality issues)?
Were there any significant quality issues during the project? If so, how were they resolved?
Reporting and Communication
How do you communicate with the rest of the team regarding progress (tools)
Progress meetings?
If yes when?
What, in general, are the main communication challenges and how are these addressed?

Were there any significant quality issues during the project?
If so, how were they resolved?
