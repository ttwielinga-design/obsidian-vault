Black Label Hospitality is a dynamic and ambitious hotel operator with a unique vision on how to 
build, expand and manage hotels. We label ourselves exclusive, due to high-quality experiences 
throughout all of our hotels. We understand the wishes of the real estate owner and our investors 
but most of all, we understand what a modern-day hotel guest is looking for.

When using the logo, always maintain a minium of ‘x’ around the logo.

B R A N D  L A B E L  U S E
The exclusive label can be used in length with text. Always make sure to leave 
space between the top of the label and the top/bottom of the text.

B R A N D  I C O N
The brand icon is stricty used on the wax stamp and can not be used for 
any other purpose and/or in combination with the logo.
