File Naming Convention – OnePager

Why it matters:

A clear naming convention ensures files are easy to find, share, version, and archive — especially when collaborating across teams or projects.

Standard Format

[YYYY-MM-DD]_[Topic/Title]_[Detail/Project/Status].[ext]
Not every file needs a full date or every field — but consistency is key.

Examples


Versioning

Use version tags in file names:
v1, v2, vFinal, vFinal_Rev1, etc.

Example:

2024-04-18_StaffingModel_v2.xlsx

Avoid

final-final-revised.xlsx
MeetingNotes.docx
Presentatie1.pptx
Budget Thomas.xlsx

Best Practices

Use underscores _ to separate fields — easier to read than spaces.
Avoid special characters (/, \, #, %, etc.) to prevent sync issues.
Keep it short but informative.
Stick to English (unless internal team agrees otherwise).
Always include a date when time-sensitive.
