## Step 3: SMART Goal Breakdown

Example:
Specific: "Write a 50,000-word book on [topic]."
Measurable: "Complete 1 chapter (5,000 words) per month."
Actionable: "Write 500 words/day, 5 days/week."
Relevant: "Supports my value of creativity and legacy."
Time-bound: "Publish by December 2025."
