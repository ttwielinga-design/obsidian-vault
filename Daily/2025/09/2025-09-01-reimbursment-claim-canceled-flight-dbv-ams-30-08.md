In short
All costs submitted were directly caused by EasyJet’s cancellation and involuntary re-
routing. They fall  within the airline’s EU261 obligations to cover reasonable accommodation, 
meals, transport, and necessary expenses until passengers are returned to their original 
destination. We therefore request full reimbursement of the above total.
