Part 1: The Questionnaire & Checklist (Individual Discovery)
Instructions: Find a quiet space and dedicate at least 90 minutes to this. Do not consult your partner. Answer with your gut first, then your head. This is about capturing your unfiltered desires and beliefs. Be brutally honest. No one achieves an exceptional life on a foundation of "shoulds."
Section A: The Life Domain – Your Personal Universe
1. Health & Vitality:
Describe your ideal state of physical health and energy in one paragraph. What does it feel like to wake up in that body?
On a scale of 1-10 (1=Not Important, 10=Absolutely Essential), how critical are the following to you?
Daily physical activity: [__]
Eating clean, whole foods: [__]
Getting 7-9 hours of sleep: [__]
Mental/emotional wellness practices (meditation, therapy, journaling): [__]
Limiting alcohol/substances: [__]
What is one health habit you refuse to compromise on?
2. Lifestyle & Environment:
Describe your perfect "average Tuesday" five years from now. Where do you wake up? What do you see, hear, smell? What is the rhythm of your day?
Check the box that best represents your ideal living environment:
[ ] Bustling major city center
[ ] Quiet, cultured urban neighborhood
[ ] Sprawling suburban home with a yard
[ ] Rural property with land and privacy
[ ] Nomadic/semi-nomadic, living in multiple places
What is the role of luxury in your life? Is it about experiences, objects, time, or something else?
3. Travel & Adventure:
On a scale, define your ideal travel style:
1 (Backpacking & Hostels) <---> 10 (Five-Star Luxury & Butler Service) [__]
What are your top 3 "must-visit" destinations in the next 10 years?
Is travel a 'nice-to-have' or a 'need-to-have' for your soul?
4. Family & Social Circle:
Do you envision having children? If so, what is your ideal timeline and number? If not, what does a child-free life of fulfillment look like?
Describe the ideal relationship with your extended family (parents, siblings). What level of involvement do you want in their lives, and vice versa?
Beyond your partner, what does a vibrant social life look like? (e.g., a few deep friendships, large group gatherings, active community involvement).
5. Personal Growth:
What skills (professional, artistic, practical) do you want to master in your lifetime?
What fears or limiting beliefs do you need to overcome to become the person you are meant to be?
What does lifelong learning look like for you? (e.g., formal education, reading, mentorship, workshops).
Section B: Business & Career Domain – Your Contribution & Wealth Engine
Beyond a title, what is the impact you want your work to have on the world?
What is your "Freedom Number" – the annual income that would allow you to live your ideal lifestyle without financial stress? $_________
On a scale, define your professional risk tolerance:
1 (Stable, secure employment) <---> 10 (All-in on my own venture) [__]
Design your ideal work-life integration:
[ ] Traditional 9-5, clear separation
[ ] Flexible hours, blended work/life
[ ] Project-based, with intense sprints and long breaks
[ ] Part-time or semi-retired
What is more important to you: professional legacy/status or time/location freedom?
Section C: The Relationship Domain – The Core Partnership
When do you feel most loved and connected to your partner? (e.g., during deep conversation, physical intimacy, shared adventure, quiet time together).
What is your primary communication style during conflict? (e.g., need to solve it now, need space to process, become quiet, become loud).
What does a thriving, passionate intimate life look like to you 10 years from now?
List your top 3 non-negotiable values in a partnership (e.g., honesty, loyalty, shared humor, mutual respect, ambition).
How should major life decisions be made? (e.g., 100% consensus, one person has final say in certain domains, etc.).
Section D: The Financial Domain – Fuel for Your Vision
What is the purpose of money for you? (e.g., security, freedom, impact, experience, power).
On a scale, define your financial risk tolerance for investing:
1 (Extremely Conservative - CDs, Bonds) <---> 10 (Highly Aggressive - Crypto, Angel Investing) [__]
What percentage of our combined income do you believe we should be saving/investing? [__]%
What is one major capital expense you envision in the next 5 years? (e.g., house down payment, starting a business, funding a sabbatical).
What is your philosophy on debt? (e.g., avoid at all costs, good for leverage like mortgages, etc.).
Section E: The Legacy Domain – Your Long-Term Impact
Standing at your 80th birthday party, what do you want people to be saying about the life you and your partner built together?
What does generosity or philanthropy look like for you? (e.g., tithing, starting a foundation, volunteering time, mentoring others).
What are your core spiritual or philosophical beliefs about life, and how do you want to express them?
What story do you want your life to tell?
