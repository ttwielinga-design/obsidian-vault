**Marketing**:

<img src="media/image4.png" style="width:4.1606in;height:2.83333in" />This means that you start by figuring out who your customer and so you can match the expectations to what the customer wants. The customer is the beginning point.

Wants are a expression of what people need.

**Demands -** The customers who have the ability that can buy what they would like to buy

Customer wants and needs are fulfilled through some combination of **tangible** and **intangible** products and services.\
\
Companies need to have a clear **value proposition**. They need to know how their products fulfill customer wants and needs.

This does not only require a careful understanding of the company’s offerings, but also a careful understanding of the **target customer** it chooses to serve.
