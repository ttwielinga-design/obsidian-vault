<th><p><strong>The big idea</strong></p>
<p>Be your own housekeeper</p></th>
<th><p><strong>User Needs/Benefits</strong></p>
<p>User/Stakeholder: Guests staying at the hotel</p>
<p>1. <strong>Who wants this?</strong> Guests who value cleanliness and want to have control over their hotel experience.</p>
<p>2. <strong>What unmet need does it serve?</strong> The unmet need is for guests to have a direct impact on the cleanliness of their room and be rewarded for their efforts. Traditional housekeeping services may not always meet the specific preferences or timing of guests, leading to a desire for a more personalized approach.</p>
<p>3. <strong>How will the user/stakeholder benefit?</strong></p>
<p>- <strong>Control</strong>: Guests can take control of their own housekeeping, ensuring their room is cleaned according to their standards and schedule.</p>
<p>- <strong>Savings</strong>: By leaving the room clean and tidy, guests earn discounts on their next stay, resulting in cost savings and increased affordability.</p>
<p>- <strong>Convenience</strong>: With the implementation of AI and cleaning technologies, the cleaning process becomes effortless and hassle-free for guests.</p>
<p>- <strong>Personalization</strong>: Guests can tailor the cleaning process to their specific needs and preferences, enhancing their overall experience.</p>
<p>- <strong>Transparency</strong>: The system promotes transparency by providing clear guidelines and incentives for guests to maintain a clean room, fostering trust and accountability.</p>
<p>Overall, guests benefit from a more customized, cost-effective, and empowering hotel experience by being their own housekeeper and utilizing AI and cleaning technologies.</p></th>

<td><p><strong>Execution</strong></p>
<p><strong>Execution:</strong></p>
<p><strong>1. How will we deliver?</strong></p>
<p>- Smart Sensors and AI: Implement smart sensors in the hotel rooms to detect cleanliness levels and provide real-time feedback to guests. Leverage AI algorithms to analyze the data and provide personalized cleaning recommendations.</p>
<p>- Automated Cleaning Systems: Invest in automated cleaning systems that can be controlled by guests through user-friendly interfaces or mobile applications. These systems should be efficient, reliable, and capable of maintaining cleanliness standards.</p>
<p>- Communication Channels: Establish effective communication channels between guests and hotel staff to address any specific cleaning needs or inquiries. This can be done through in-room tablets, mobile apps, or dedicated customer service representatives.</p>
<p>2. <strong>What asset or capability does this leverage or require?</strong></p>
<p>- Technology Infrastructure: Develop and maintain a robust technological infrastructure that supports the integration of smart sensors, AI algorithms, and automated cleaning systems. This requires expertise in IoT (Internet of Things), AI development, and data analytics.</p>
<p>- Cleaning Technologies: Acquire or partner with cleaning technology providers to obtain state-of-the-art automated cleaning systems that meet the desired standards of efficiency and effectiveness.</p>
<p>- User Experience Design: Invest in user experience design to create intuitive interfaces and seamless interactions for guests to control and monitor the cleaning process.</p>
<p>3. <strong>What partners do we need?</strong></p>
<p>- Cleaning Technology Providers: Collaborate with manufacturers or suppliers of automated cleaning systems to ensure the availability of cutting-edge equipment and ongoing support for maintenance and upgrades.</p>
<p>- Technology Experts: Form partnerships with technology experts specializing in AI, IoT, and data analytics to develop and optimize the smart sensor and AI algorithms.</p>
<p>- Hospitality Industry Experts: Engage with professionals from the hospitality industry to gain insights into guest preferences, cleaning standards, and best practices in order to tailor the offering effectively.</p>
<p>By leveraging smart technology, collaborating with relevant partners, and investing in the necessary capabilities, we can successfully execute the concept of guests being their own housekeeper with AI and cleaning technologies in the hotel.</p></td>
