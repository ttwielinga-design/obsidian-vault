During the online company talks on February 16<sup>th</sup> I attended the following companies:

1.  Accor

2.  Hilton

3.  Hyatt

4.  Marriott

From the Accor presentation I got a very good impression. I really liked the fact that they held an interactive quiz to keep us sharp and that we were able to ask question all the way through the quiz. I thought Hilton and Hyatt were very interesting regarding the importance they put on the well being of their employees as well as the fact that they have special apps to help employees and guests grow and relax. For Marriott what I found very interesting was the sustainability aspect, as well as what they mentioned during the talk about how they try to help all their employees as best as possible to full fill their fullest potential.

For my choice in internship the Marriott presentation was so convincing that I actually changed my preference for Fort Lauderdale from the Ritz to W hotel in Fort Lauderdale.
