The reason why I chose for coconut to be the star ingredient of my dish is because I before this assignment didn’t really know how to cook with coconut. This is why I decided to do more research in to it. And I found out that it is actually a really healthy product and also easy to work with. Furthermore, I thought it would be fun to try to incorporate something into my dish that I have never heard of or cooked with: and turn coconut into something that looks and also tastes a bit like bacon. It is a healthier and better alternative to the real bacon and actually tastes really good.
