What is a competence?

First let’s look at what different kinds of competencies there are. There are three core competencies. The communication competency, the thinking competency and the personal & social competency.\`

Communication competency is important to exchange and gain information, ideas and experiences.

The thinking competency is all about knowledge, skills and processes we associate with intellectual development. You can see it threw creative and critical thinking.

The personal & social competency includes social awareness, cultural identity, social emotional health & well being and being social responsible.

So you could say that competence is the road that you need to follow to get to the point where you want to go.
