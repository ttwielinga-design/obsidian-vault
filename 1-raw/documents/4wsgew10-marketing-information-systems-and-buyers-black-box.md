<td><p>In this workshop you will study a company’s marketing information systems by looking at its social media marketing. You will learn social media can be used for both promotion and for research.</p>
<p>Rather than performing marketing research, you will analyse what a company’s social media messaging can reveal about its market insights.</p>
<p>You will also learn to approach marketing from the consumer’s point of view. To achieve this you will learn about the buyer decision process and will use the Consumer’s black box to analyse this. Furthermore, you will study the consumer’s cultural, social, personal and psychological characteristics in order to fully understand the consumer buying behaviour.</p></td>
