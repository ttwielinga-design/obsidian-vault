Slide 3: Meta analysis

A meta-analysis is a statistical technique that combines the results of multiple studies to arrive at a more accurate and reliable estimate of the effect of an intervention or treatment.

The advantages of meta-analysis include:

- Increased statistical power

- Greater accuracy

- Summary of findings

The Disadvantages:

- Time-consuming

- Complex statistical techniques
