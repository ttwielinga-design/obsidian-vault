<td>Pure vanilla extract</td>
</tr>
<tr>

<td>All-purpose flower</td>
</tr>

<td colspan="2" style="text-align: center;">7g</td>
<td>Salt</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td colspan="2" style="text-align: center;">7g</td>
<td>Cinnamon</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;"> </td>
<td colspan="2" style="text-align: center;">Preparation method: </td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;"> </td>
<td style="text-align: center;">1 </td>
<td>Warm milk in a pan until it is at 40C</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;"> </td>
<td style="text-align: center;">2 </td>
<td> Put the milk in a bowl and add 15g of sugar and ad the yeast. Mix for 1 min and after that let it sit for 5 minutes</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;"></td>
<td style="text-align: center;">3 </td>
<td>Add the sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla to the mixture in the bowl. Use the mixer until it is a nice and smooth dough.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;"> </td>
<td style="text-align: center;">4 </td>
<td> Add the flour, salt and cinnamon and start mixing again start at a low power and build it up till full.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;"></td>
<td style="text-align: center;">5</td>
<td>The dough should be smooth and only slightly tacky to the touch. If the dough seems too sticky and is not forming a ball, add more flour until a smooth ball is formed.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;"></td>
<td style="text-align: center;">6</td>
<td>Transfer dough to a floured surface and knead with your hands until it is smooth and elastic (about 3-5 minutes). Form it into a ball.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;"></td>
<td style="text-align: center;">7</td>
<td>Make the dough into a ball and then place it into a bowl and put a damp towel over it and let the dough rise. Let it sit in a warm place for an hour</td>
</tr>
</tbody>

<th colspan="2" style="text-align: center;">Making the Cinnamon rolls: </th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;">1</td>
<td> Sprinkle a large surface with flour</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;">2</td>
<td> Press the gas out of the dough</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;">3</td>
<td> Roll the dough into a 60 by 30 cm rectangle</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;">4</td>
<td>Spread the filling all over the dough using greased hands or a greased spatula.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;">5</td>
<td> Roll up dough cut into 12 equal sized rolls</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;">6</td>
<td> Put the rolls onto a lightly greased baking tray. Then let it rise for 40 min. And preheat the oven till 180C</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;">7</td>
<td>Bake them in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 18-20 minutes.</td>
