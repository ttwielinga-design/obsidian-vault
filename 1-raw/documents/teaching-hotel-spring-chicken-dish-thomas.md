> Equipment

- Smoker

- Fry Pan 2x

- Rope

Method:

CHICKEN

1.  Preheat the smoker until it is at 135C

2.  Season the chicken with oil, Pepper powder and Rosemary. Then tie the legs together and tuck the wing tips behind the shoulder joint.

3.  Put the chicken in the smoker for 2,5 to 3 hours. Make sure the breast reaches 70C and the thighs are at 80C internal temp.

4.  Remove the chicken from the smoker

> WILDMUSHROOMS

1.  Dice the Shallots and the garlic.

2.  Put some olive oil in the pan make sure it is nice and hot.

3.  Put the shallots in the pan mix them up till they get a nice bit of color then throw in the garlic

4.  After about 2 minutes put in the mushrooms

5.  Bake them for 5 minutes then take them off the heat.

> JERUSALEM ARTICHOKE

1.  Peel them, then cut them into chunks.

2.  Place them in an oiled frying pan and fry on a medium heat until golden on both sides.

3.  Put the herbs in the pan, pepper salt and a splash of white wine

4.  Put the lid on the pan and let it simmer for 20 to 25 min.

5.  Remove the lid get them on the choppingboard and thinly slice them

PLATE:

1.  Cut the chicken in nice thin slices

2.  Put the mushrooms, onions and garlic at the botem

3.  On top of this put the sliced chicken

4.  And finish with the Artichoke around the plate
