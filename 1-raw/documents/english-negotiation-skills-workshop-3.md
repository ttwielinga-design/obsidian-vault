English negotiation skills workshop 3

Quiz master questions

1.  Give me three ways to present a point

    1.  Allow me to give the floor to

    2.  I’d like to call on … to present his views on

    3.  … Would you care to comment?

    4.  I think … would like to make a point

2.  What is a way to summarize a meeting?

    1.  In short …

    2.  To sum up, there seems to be

    3.  Briefly,

    4.  If I may just go over the points given so far

3.  How do you completely agree with someone?

    1.  I completely agree

    2.  I’m in total agreement

    3.  I’m of exactly the same opinion

4.  Neutral agreement

    1.  I agree

    2.  I think you are right

    3.  I think we can accept your possision on that

5.  How to tacticly disagree

    1.  I agree up to a point …

    2.  You have a point there but,

    3.  I can see your point of view, but surely

    4.  I have some sympathy with your posision

6.  Fill in the setance

    1.  It … to me that …

    2.  It seems to me that

7.  If I … I’d like to … my … Ms. Wang to … to that

    1.  If I may I’d like to ask my collegue Ms. Wang to reply to that.

8.  How do you sum up what you said?

    1.  Well if I could just sum up the disscustion
