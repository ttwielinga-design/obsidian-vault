**Fellow student**

1\. Describe me in no more than 50 words.

Thomas is a very social guy, who cannot stop talking. He always knows or wants to know everything about something that interest him. He is also up to drink beers with friends and have a good time. Thomas is never craggy and most of the time smiling.

2\. What are my strengths (mention at least two)?

\- Always being capable of talking to other people and keep conversations going.

\- Discipline. If Thomas needs to do something, he will do it as soon as possible

3\. Describe specific situations in which I have demonstrated these strengths.

\- When we used to go out to clubs, Thomas was always capable of making small talk to everyone he just met. Also when he was in a new class at the beginning of a semester, he was talking to everyone left right and center.

\- I remember how Thomas always did his homework etc. first before doing fun things. And if there was a day where he was doing fun things right after school, he would be doing homework or projects in the evening and sometimes in the night. But it was finished way before it was due.

4\. What are my weaknesses (mention at least two)? In what specific situation were these noticeable?

\- Being clear when using text or different types of social media. When using social media to communicate with people, Thomas has a hard time of being clear. He is always asked to explain himself or to retype his messages. This is probably because he has dyslexia, but even with that it is a real weakness.

\- Thomas has a thing where he speaks before he thinks. In some cases it is really funny but sometimes it is really annoying. There was this moment where out of kindness he said he was willing to pick a friend up in Eindhoven when he was going to Zeeland instead of picking up this friend in Roosendaal at the train station. From Utrecht to Eindhoven to Zeeland is just a very dumb and unnecessary drive.

5\. What aspect should I improve first?

Thomas should improve on the second weakness I explained on the question above.

6\. Do you have any tips on how I could do this?

I would suggest him, that he would wait a few seconds before speaking and think about if what he is gonna say is a good thing and not just dumb.
