**Evaluation form (mid-term and final evaluation)**

**Operational Internship**

*Complete the dedicated sections in this document. For this, please use the **Tab function** from your keyboard to jump in the document from one section to the other and your **Mouse** to select the appropriate box for drop-down list.*

**<u>Objective:</u>** During the internship, a student has two official moments to reflect on her/his performance and get feedback from the company mentor. The objective of these evaluations is to know where the student stands and what she/he should focus on in the remaining months of the internship and the development afterwards.

**<u>Timing:</u>** The student will carry out a mid-term evaluation at around the midway point of the internship (before the HMSM internship supervisor visit or conference call, and this may be well before the exact middle!). The 2<sup>nd</sup> evaluation should take place towards the end of the internship.

**<u>Procedure:</u>**

For each evaluation (mid-term and final evaluation), the student should proceed as follows:

- Step 1: The students takes the initiative and schedules the meetings with the company mentor and him/herself and the one with the company mentor, him/herself and the internship school supervisor

- Step 2: The student completes this evaluation form (self-evaluation)

- Step 3: The student asks her/his company mentor to complete this evaluation form (please hand-in a blank copy of this document for the mid-term evaluation)

- Step 4: The student and company mentor meet to review both evaluation forms (self-evaluation and evaluation completed by the company mentor), compare differences and discuss the student’s performance

- Step 5: The student uploads both evaluation forms (<u>self-evaluation and a scanned copy of company evaluation with the company mentor signatures and stamp</u>) in OnStage in the corresponding activity (mid-term or final evaluation)

<u>Note</u>: for the final evaluation, the same process as above will apply. The only difference is that the student will take the mid-term <u>self-evaluation</u> form and complete it with her/his final self-evaluation. The company mentor will take the mid-term evaluation and complete with his/her final evaluation. Like this, we can see the progresses made.
