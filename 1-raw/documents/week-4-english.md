**FLAWT: you can just taste the freshness**

*From a horrid hotel with no F&B to putting themselves on the “foodie” map.*

FLAWT as a hotel may have had some issues in the past but during the last year they have turned it around. Starting this year they are introducing a new menu. The theme of this menu is all green and biological.

The food not be too complicated and like I just mentioned it will entirely focus on being sustainable.

For the starters you can choose from a red beetroot carpaccio with goat cheese, cucumber, roasted nut crumble and balsamic syrup, or if you are feeling more fancy you can choose the lamb smoke carpaccio marinaded in red wine with quail eggs, walnuts, green beans and shallot mouse.

As a main course you have three options risotto with roasted pumpkin (for all my vegans out there), secondly a sea bass with back venus rice, and lastly a veal cheek with chicory. Boy was the last one good. You the meat was just so tender and lushes on that veal cheek.

And if you want to finish the night with a bang as a desert I would recommend the hazelnut cake with lemon cremeux.

If you are not in the “fancy” mood they also have an amazing bistro where you can order (vegan) burgers, nachos, and other small or large bites. Next to that they also have a bar where you can choose from a plethora of cocktails.

I personally really enjoyed tasting this beautiful new menu, and I would highly recommend you to go there for an amazing tasting. Even if you are just in the neighborhood you should go check it out.
