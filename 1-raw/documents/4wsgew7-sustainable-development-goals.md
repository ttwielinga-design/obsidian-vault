<td><p>Sustainability is becoming an increasingly crucial component within the Hospitality Industry. Many, if not all hotels have adopted some form of sustainability and adhere to targets outlined by the 17 Sustainable Development Goals. But what exactly is sustainability? And how can these 17 sustainable development goals be applied in practice?</p>
<p>In this workshop, we will dive deeper into the concept and principles of sustainability, such as the triple bottom line and the 5P’s of sustainable development. Aside from discussing the prepared homework assignment, we will practice in recognizing which sustainable development goals match relevant current issues as well as discuss various ways in which a hotel can contribute to the targets set by these 17 SDG’s.</p></td>
</tr>
