**Aims and Objectives of the Literature Review:**

**Aim:**

The aim of this literature review is to comprehensively examine the challenges faced by middle managers in various professional contexts, with a specific focus on the "sandwiched middle" position they occupy. This review seeks to gain insights into the unique role and identity of middle managers and how they navigate their responsibilities within organizations.

**Objectives:**

*Exploring Middle Management Identity:*

To analyze the concept of middle management identity, drawing insights from the metaphor of middle managers as "umbrella carriers" and considering the implications of this identity for their role and actions (Beth R. Crisp, 2022).

*Understanding Middle Managerial Work:*

To explore the experiences of middle managers in their role and investigate how they balance the expectations and pressures from senior management above and subordinates below (S. Gjerde, 2020).

*Middle Management Challenges in Professional Contexts:*

To examine the challenges and complexities faced by middle managers in professional settings such as academic institutions and consider how their role may differ from middle management in other contexts (S. Gjerde, 2020).

*Implications of Middle Management Practices on Organizational Wellbeing:*

To investigate the impact of middle management practices on organizational wellbeing and employee mental health, with a focus on the role of middle managers in protecting subordinates and their own wellbeing (S. Gjerde, 2020; Z. Bajorek, 2020).

*Middle Managerial Wellbeing and Organizational Productivity:*

To assess the relationship between middle manager wellbeing and organizational productivity, taking into account the role of middle managers in enhancing employee wellbeing and managing academic workloads (Z. Bajorek, 2020; S. Gjerde, 2020).

*Recommendations for Enhancing Middle Management Effectiveness:*

To identify best practices and recommendations for improving the effectiveness of middle managers in professional contexts, including strategies to alleviate the challenges they face and promote their wellbeing (Z. Bajorek, 2020; S. Gjerde, 2020).

By addressing these objectives, this literature review will aim to provide a comprehensive understanding of middle management, shedding light on their roles, challenges, and contributions to success.
