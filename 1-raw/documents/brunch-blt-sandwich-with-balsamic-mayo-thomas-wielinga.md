<td>Aged balsamic vinegar</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;"> </td>
<td colspan="2" style="text-align: center;">Preparation method: </td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;"> </td>
<td style="text-align: center;">1 </td>
<td> Combine the egg and balsamic vinegar in a blender, and process on low speed until smooth.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;"> </td>
<td style="text-align: center;">2 </td>
<td> While blending, slowly drizzle in a few drops of grapeseed oil until the mayonnaise begins to emulsify, then continue slowly pouring the oil in a thin stream until it is all added. The mayonnaise should be smooth and shiny.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="text-align: center;"> </td>
<td style="text-align: center;">3 </td>
<td> Season the mayonnaise with salt</td>
</tr>
