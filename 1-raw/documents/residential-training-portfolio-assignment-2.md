**The step-by-step analysis**

**Phase 1: the situation and the dilemma**

The ethical dilemma that I chose to talk about is as followed. One of my best friends struggles with school a lot, not because he is stupid but because he lacks intrinsic motivation. All the subjects came easy to him except for one essential part that you need to complete for you diploma, his PWS.

After first going to Highschool in Utrecht for 5 years he got kicked out there because he would have had to repeat VWO 4 for the second time which is not allowed. So he went to the “avondschool” and started there in the school year of 2018 – 2019. That year he did the exams for HAVO 5 and passed all his classes, with flying colors, except for his PWS, which he didn’t even attempt to hand in. When the next school year came around (2019 -2020) he only had to hand in his PWS and get a 4.5 and then he would be finished with school. That year he didn’t even attempt to hand something in eighter. In early march of 2020 I tried and sat down with him to try to help him to the best of my abilities, but this just didn’t help, I was practically writing his PWS for him with him sitting next to me, so I gave up after some time because I felt that he didn’t want it enough and I wasn’t going to write an entirely new PWS. Near the end of spring 2020 I had a talk with him about ambition, I had talks about this subject before but this was different. After some time I asked him if he was happy with where he is now, working at the Jumbo fulltime and for the rest sitting on the couch and playing football. He told me he wasn’t but that he just could never find the motivation or the support to start and finish this project. He said that he wanted to do accountancy in Den Haag, and study there. So to me this to me sounded like he just needed to get over this hurdle and move on with his life.

This is where for me a dilemma arose. I saw that a dear friend of mine needed help, and just had to finish this chapter of his life to move on. I had two choices here: Do nothing, or give him my PWS, which I knew was good because I got a 9 for it on VWO level.

The ethical dilemma here is whether it is fine to give my friend my PWS to make sure that he can move on with his studies and/or life. I know that it is against the law, and plagiarism to give it to him but if I don’t he will most likely never move forward.

The factual information here is that a person wants to move on with his life but can’t, because he can’t find the motivation to finish his PWS. I have a good PWS, and I know that were he to hand it in he will get a passed grade.

The normative assertion here would be split, because some people would say that it is fine to give him my work so that he can move further and others will say that it is not moral, and that other people work hard to get their HAVO diploma and so should he.

The subjective assertion here is that I would say: I’ll just give it to him so that he is free and he can move further. Furthermore he is my friend and my gut feeling is that no one will ever know and he gets his HAVO diploma.

**Phase 2: The analysis**

Like I said above my ethical dilemma is whether I should give my friend my PWS or not.

As stated before I have two main courses of action: give it to him or not give it to him. Another course of action could also be that I sit next to him and help him make his PWS, but as stated earlier I already have gone that road before and it didn’t work out so well that time so I doubt whether that it will help this time. Never the less it is still an option.

The norms and values that are involved are: honesty, because it is not honest to hand in my work whilst other people work hard for it and he is just ‘getting’ it. Since this is plagiarism I know that it is cheating and against the law. There is also a normative problem here due to the fact that it is not normal for people to use other people’s work for their own benefit (without citation).

The interests for me are that a dear friend of mine finally going to get his diploma and move forward with his life. This to me is important because I can about my friends and will do pretty much everything for the people I care about.

The interests for my friend are that he gets his diploma can go ahead and do accountancy move away from home and finally be free of this task that is above his head.

There is no real interest for other people. The only thing that may be of effect here is that the rest of society may see this as a ‘bad’ thing because if everyone did this that would mean that there is no use of putting in the work for other people because you can just copy it and have a nice PWS.

As for the aspects of the code of conduct (CoC) that play a part in this dilemma there are a few. The first aspect that is at play here is that in the CoC it states that you should behave as a curious student and eager to learn which entails having done adequate preparations. This part is for sure a risk here since my friend wouldn’t have prepared anything and this it would be at fault.

The aspect of taking responsibility is also a problem here since I here am not taking responsibility for my actions and am allowing someone to copy my work. In this case I am also not showing exemplary behavior because it is wrong to give someone your work and it should most definitely not be copied.

**Phase 3: The decision-making**

For me the norms and values that are the most important to me as I stated before is friendship, this is also the main reason I did what I did. However the reason why this was a real dilemma for me is because I also rate honesty really high. For me these two values clashed hard. The reason I in the end made the decision to give my friend my PWS is because I truly didn’t see any other option. He is my friend and I wanted to help him. It was important to me to help him.

I also concluded another friend of mine about this and he told me that he understood where I was coming from. He told me that the big distinction for him also was that it is a really good friend and he too would do anything for a friend in need. He however did say that he would make sure that if I were to do it my name would not be on the file what so ever to make sure it can not be linked back.

In the end I did give him PWS and I am happy to say that my friend is now doing accountancy. I am really happy with my choice and know that for some people my discission was dubious to say the least but for me it was crystal-clear and I don’t regret it what so ever.**\**
