**SCQA – Chapter 1**

The Penta Hotel Wiesbaden is located in the historic spa town of Wiesbaden, 2 kilometers from the Wiesbaden Hauptbahnhof (main train station) and 20 kilometers from Frankfurt Airport. The hotel has 209 rooms over six floors. It attracts both business and leisure travelers as it provides convenient access to many of the city’s main attractions and amenities. The hotel is part of the Penta Hotel group which consists of over 20 hotels across Europe. Up until 2019, Penta Hotel Wiesbaden showed an upward trend in revenues. Since the coronavirus pandemic in 2020, the hotel landscape has changed drastically. This has also impacted the Penta Hotel in Wiesbaden. This has raised the question. What issues is Penta Hotel Wiesbaden currently facing because of this change in landscape? From this question we came to six major strategic issues.

1.  How can the Penta Living Room concept become more economically sustainable?

2.  How the high convention rates help take advantage of the fact that Wiesbaden is a railway junction?

3.  How can the Penta Neighborhood concept be improved to make it more economically sustainable?

4.  How can the restaurant seating area be restructured to make sure that competitors with similar facilities do not out compete us?

5.  How can Penta's restaurant seating be restructured to reduce the recovery time after COVID?

6.  How can the conference rooms be updated to make them more economically sustainable?

In the following six chapters these six claims are going to be substantiated using quantitative and qualitative analysis.
