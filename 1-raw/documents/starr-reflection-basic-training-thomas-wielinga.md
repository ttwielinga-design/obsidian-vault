One thing that I think Juliette could still improve on is that she should also take into account important hand in assignments or other exams. **S.T.A.R.R. reflection goal 1: I will take out the trash every time it is full and I will help with cleaning the kitchen even if I didn’t use anything.**

**Situation –** The situation before was that in our hallway the trash can was pretty much always full. This was irritating for everyone and on one wanted to do it.

**Task** – The task I set for myself was to take out the trash every time it is full and I will help with cleaning the kitchen even if I didn’t use anything.

**Action** – What I did the last few weeks was exactly as I said in my goal. I tried to clean the trash every time it was full and helped cleaning the kitchen. There were moments it was really irritating. And to be honest I didn’t do it a couple of times, but in the end, I did see a change in habits because now this last week I didn’t hesitate anymore to take out the trash and this really surprised me. I actually only noticed it just now while writing this it really just got into my system.

**Results** – The result of this goal is that now the living room looks cleaner and everyone in the hallway is benefitting from it. There are no more over loaded trash cans and the kitchen, most of the time, is actually clean.

**Reflection** – Looking back at this goal I am glad I set it. This is because it showed me that if I put in the effort eventually it becomes second nature and everyone around you appreciates the effort that you put into keeping your community clean.

The feedback I got from my fellow students was really good. They are so happy that I set this goal because everything in our hall way is much cleaner now. I did however also get told that I should be careful with doing too much because that can cause other people in the house to get lazier and expect me to do everything. This I think was a good point that I hadn’t thought off before setting this goal. Maybe I should have taken this into a count and said that everyone should help more. And that that should have been my goal.

**S.T.A.R.R. reflection goal 2: I will always make clear planning for things I have to do at school and make sure within this planning I am always finished one day before the due date.**

**Situation** – Before I set this goal I was, I felt, not working as efficiently as I could be. This is because I didn’t make clear planning’s and I did my work last minute mostly. So, I wanted to change that through this goal.

**Task** – The task for me was to start sooner on assignments and make a clear planning of when I should do things so that I don’t have stress when handing in assignments.

**Action** – The action that I took was that I made planning’s for my exam week, which worked because I passed. And I stared earlier on assignments that I had to hand in.

**Result** – The result of this goal was that I worked more organized. This is seen in the grades that I achieved during the exam week but also in the time that I put in versus what I got out of it. Because I worked and planned efficiently, I didn’t have to cram everything in my head in the last 2 or 3 days I did it in the spread of 3 weeks which is for sure a welcome change in my book.

**Reflecting** – I think that I accomplished this goal partly. This is because I do start sooner on my assignments, tests, and homework. But I haven’t fully mastered it yet. An example of this is actually this assignment. I designed to do 80% of it as preparation for the lessons that we had and the composing of the assignment I left to the last minute this is because I thought that I deserved a break after the exams well knowing that this would only take 40 minutes so I probably should have just done it straight away.

The feedback that I got from my fellow students was that I did a good job on this goal. I should however think more a head and be more determent about achieving the goal because in the long run you want to build a habit because those actually stay.
