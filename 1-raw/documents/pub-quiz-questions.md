1.  Venezuela is home to the world's highest waterfall, the Angel Falls. How did they get that name?

<!-- -->

1.  After the visit of a pilot who spotted it from the air and landed there

2.  After the Indian queen Angelixa Alpaxi who ruled the area

3.  From the local belief that an Angel stands guard of the falls

4.  <span class="mark">After the original discoverer of the falls "Juan Angel de la frontera"</span>

Explanation- Explorer Ernesto Sanchez La Cruz, was the first to find the falls but they were not well known across the world until 1933 when American pilot, Jimmy Angel, spotted them while searching for a mineral bed. In 1936 he returned and landed his plane at the top of the falls and the falls were named after him.
