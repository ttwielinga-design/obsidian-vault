<td style="text-align: left;"><p>Once you have conducted interviews, you are faced with a big amount of textual data. When analysing this data, it is important to work in a structured way. This process is called <em>coding</em>.</p>
<p>In this session we will discuss the various steps in the coding process; and, by means of appetizer (before you start working on your own data), we will practice the coding technique on a small representative dataset.</p></td>
