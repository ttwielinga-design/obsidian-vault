Self-reflection gastronomic lunch – Thomas Wielinga

Within this project there were a lot of little problems that occurred. This was sometimes due to the way we didn’t set clear hand in dates and or unclear information back and forth. The tasks that I was given within this project were: to do the dogfish, this includes making the recipe doing the research and making a food cost calculation for the dish. While doing these tasks there were some obstacles that I had to overcome. One of the main obstacles was that I had no real in-depth knowledge on fish and on how to build up a dish. This I solved by doing my own research and learning form videos online and articles. The result of this was that after I put in this time and effort I immediately saw a difference in speed and efficiency within my work on the dish. I am really glad I put in that time. Reflecting on what I did maybe next time I start on a project I should make a schedule for myself so I have a better overview of what I need to do in said time. Never the less I really enjoyed this project and my teammates and had a lot of fun doing this project.
