When negotiating remember that: negotiators are people first. Negotiating with someone if you have a good personal relation ship is going to be easier than if you don’t have a good relation ship with somebody. Furthermore, there are two types of interests for a negotiator: you have the substance and the relationship. As a negotiator you are trying to satisfy both. Using positional bargaining can put relationship, and substance in conflict but if you don’t care about one it can be very useful to get results fast. This will lead to a trade of between relationship and substance this can be avoided by stating clear boundaries before a negotiation. If the negotiations are not going very well maybe try to put yourself in their shoes because then you can understand their point of view, and their perception.
