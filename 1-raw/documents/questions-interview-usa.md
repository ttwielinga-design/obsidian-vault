**Questions Interview Florida**

**Stereotypes:**

1.  Did you have any prejudgments when moving to the USA?

    1.  What were they?

    2.  Were they confirmed or are they generalized?

**General questions:**

1.  What were the cultural differences you were already aware of when moving to the USA?

2.  Are there many different cultures/ethnicities in the place you live?

    1.  Which ones were the most prominent?

**Diversity**

1.  Do you think that hotels within the US focus on creating a diverse work environment?

    1.  How did you experience this?

    2.  When looking at the main departments, could you sketch us a brief overview of how the teams looked regarding cultures/ethnicities?

**Different aspects**

1.  How did you experience masculinity at the workplace (e.g., gender roles \[men should do this, women should do that\])?

    1.  And how was this with in private life

    2.  What was the difference, if any, to your own frame of reference (the Netherlands)?

2.  What do you think of the distance of power within the company that you work at?

    1.  Are the managers easy to reach if you had a complaint or suggestion? (If you are a manager, do you think you are easy to reach?)

3.  Did you ever give feedback to your employees at your work or did you ever receive feedback?

    1.  What is the difference when giving/receiving feedback when comparing it to \[your country of origin\] / \[ the Netherlands?\]

4.  What do you think of the Long- versus Short-term orientation with in your workplace?

    - *Long-term: good and evil are relative, knows how to adapt, traditions can be changed*

    - *Short-term: good and evil are absolute, someone who is always the same, traditions are there to be kept*

<!-- -->

5.  Generally speaking, do you think the USA is more individualistic than \[your country of origin\] / \[ the Netherlands?\]?

    1.  And why do you think that is? How can you notice the difference?

**Low- versus High-context:**

1.  How does the management structure look like at the Place you work at look?

2.  When making decisions who makes them and why?

3.  How does having a disagreement with a coworker play out?

    1.  Is it confrontational? \[head on\] (do you not talk about it?)

**Time**

1.  When someone invites you over for dinner in the USA when do you arrive? (10 minutes early, on time, 10 minutes late, etc.)

2.  When you have a business meeting in the USA when do you arrive?

**Space**

1.  When waiting in a line how far do you stand from the other person?

2.  When talking to someone in a bar how close do you sit / stand

3.  When talking to your friends in a bar how close do you sit / stand?

**Communication**

1.  When communicating with other people do, they use more/less/same amount of body langue than \[your country of origin\] / \[ the Netherlands?\]?

**Salaries and wages:**

1.  How would you describe the average salaries with hospitality in the USA when comparing it with the salaries in \[your country of origin\] / \[ the Netherlands?\]?

2.  Can you explain to us how tipping works in the USA?

3.  Could you describe your work to private life to us (work-life balance in general)?

    1.  Do you hang out with your colleagues after work?
