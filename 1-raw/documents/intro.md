We have composed two very different dishes. The first one is a marinaded dogfish with a parsnip purée, pickled cucumbers, and deep-fried dried skin dogfish crisp. The second dish is the Asian turbot which consists of a pan-fried Turbot, a beurre blanc sauce, oil of wasabi, cassava crisps, and shiitake. Both of theses dishes are very different in taste yet still we believe we have managed to get all the CFS’s in the dish and onto the plate. Hope you will enjoy reading it!
