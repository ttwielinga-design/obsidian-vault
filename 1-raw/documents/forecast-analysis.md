Forecast analysis

We projected a total revenu of 10,6 milion dolars for our year 3 budget. This is based on the growth that we have had within our rooms, F&B and other operating departments over the last 2 years. Every department in year 3 will see a significant increase in revenue. Below we will break the revenues down step by step.

Revenues

RDM

During the last 2 years we have almost tripled our revenue when compared to year 0 we feel that this growth will continue but in a slightly less exponential way. Below you can read our budgeted prices for the rooms.

| **Budgeted Price** | **Average** |
|--------------------|-------------|
| Weekday Standard   | \$162.87    |
| Weekend Standard   | \$162.78    |
| Weekday Executive  | \$234.23    |
| Weekend Executive  | \$232.61    |

We also expect to sell around 23000 standard rooms and 3000 executive rooms. Compared to year 2 this will be a significant increase. To relies these higher room sales we decided to lower the price a little bit in order to keep an occupancy of at least 75%.

For our pricing strategy we decided to go with occupancy based. Since for our target segment this will go the best.

Contracts

We decided not to go for any contracts this year and we didn’t do so in any previous years.

Events, conferences, and Groups

In year 2 we sold a lot of conferences and in year 3 we projected that we will sell even more. Since we are a business hotel to us it is important that our conference rooms are fully booked year round, therefore we went with relatively lower prices to again retain that high occupancy.

F&B

For F&B we saw that there was real potential for growth when comparing it to year 2 if we kept seasonality in to account more there for instead of using just one price for the entire year we decided to fluctuate it depending on the seasonality. For F&B we came to a total projected revenue of \$3.410.772,-

Other operating departments

For our other operating departments we expected it to increase but no by a lot since we don’t really offer a lot of extras there for we went with an increase of 20000 when comparing it to year 2

Expenses

Direct Expenses on the Rooms Schedule

The employees that clean the standard and executive rooms are paid with band 2, and no changes will be made there. For training we decided to maintain the same amount per trainings for all the departments so we will keep investing the maximum amount.

Direct expenses F&B

For F&B personal we don’t expect there to be any major changes since our hotel will be going for around the same amount of bookings per evening and thus menus sold. We decided to keep it at \$580.000,-

Direct Expenses on the Other Operated Departments

Since we don’t project much growth we decided that the expenses were going to be % the same as the year before.

Administration & General – Other expenses
