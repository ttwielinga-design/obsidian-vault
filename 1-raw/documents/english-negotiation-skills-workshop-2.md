How to take notes:

1.  Be prepared – select a good seat where u can hear everything

2.  Take hand written notes – you remember these better

3.  Structure your notes – try to always structure in the same way

4.  Be selective – don’t write down everything

    1.  Topics covered (should be part of the agenda)

    2.  Action items

    3.  Decisions

    4.  Items that you don’t have time for that need to be “parked” and followed up later

    5.  Questions that need to be answered

    6.  Key facts (though not if they are documented somewhere else)

5.  Use symbols and abbreviations – this is to shorten your notes

Notes from: Effective Meetings in Plain English

- Before the meeting ask yourself: Is this meeting necessary?

- Plan the agenda of the meeting:

  - Purpose

  - Time

  - Items

  - Process

- Only invite key people

- Send the agenda of the meeting a few days before the meeting (prepared)

- Book the meeting room early and make sure you have all the equipment

- At the meeting work through the agenda item by item

- Establish ground rules for the meeting

Notes: How to run an effective meeting - 4. Running meetings

- Your role is to get the best our of your team

- Get everyone to contribute – just because someone is quite doesn’t meat they don’t have any thoughts on the matter

Notes: How to Effectively Run a Meeting – insteps

1.  Get specific – goal

    1.  Deal with bad news - asap

2.  Write an agenda for your meeting

3.  Set a time for the meeting

4.  Have everyone silence their phones

5.  Keep the meeting focused on the topic at hand

6.  Make sure everyone participates

7.  Keep the peace

8.  End your meeting – when goals are met

9.  Follow up

Notes: 7 Secrets to running effective meetings

1.  Make your objective clear

2.  Start on time and end on time

3.  Send out and agenda, and any materials to the attendece before the meeting

4.  Try to keep the number of people – only call those connected with the topic

5.  Take notes

6.  Clearly identify decisions

7.  Use the last minute to recap
