Dietitians’ corner
******************

What should I eat before a cookout so I don't overdo it?

“Eating a balanced meal or snack beforehand, something with
protein, fiber, and healthy fats, can help you arrive at the
cookout feeling satisfied rather than overly hungry. That said,
holiday cookouts are meant to be enjoyed, and tuning into your
hunger and fullness cues can help you find a comfortable balance.
Focusing on enjoying the food and the company often matters more
than trying to control every bite.”

Emily Sullivan, RD

MyFitnessPal Registered Dietitian
