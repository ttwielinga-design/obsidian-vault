Should I marinate meat before cooking?

“I personally love to marinate meat before cooking, especially
when grilling. You can get creative with flavor combinations
featuring dried spices and fresh herbs mixed with oil, vinegar or
citrus juice all helping to pack in flavor to the final meal. In
a time crunch? Marinating for as little as 20 - 30 minutes can be
a great place to get started! Or, get a head start on tomorrow’s
meal and allow the meat to marinate overnight in the fridge
before cooking for even more flavor.”

Melissa Jaeger, RD, LD

Head of Nutrition, MyFitnessPal
