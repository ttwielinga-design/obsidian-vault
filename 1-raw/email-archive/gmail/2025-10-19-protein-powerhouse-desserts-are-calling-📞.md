Have a sweet tooth but trying to dodge the dreaded sugar crash?
Enter protein.

There’s a reason protein is trending. It plays a key role in
building and maintaining lean muscle, supporting bone health,
immune function, balanced blood sugar, and supporting weight
management.

With just a few simple swaps, you can elevate the protein in your
favorite treats, and voilà; dessert is back on the menu.

Why should I add protein to my dessert?

“Eating a sweet treat with protein gives you the sweetness you
crave from a dessert without the excess sugar that can lead to a
crash later. A protein-packed dessert may also help you skip
mindless snacking, putting you one step closer to your goals.”

Joanna Gregg

MyFitnessPal Registered Dietitian
