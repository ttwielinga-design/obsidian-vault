# Important Notice: Coinbase System Maintenance

**From:** Coinbase
**Date:** 2025-10-15

> On October 25, Coinbase will be temporarily under maintenance and unavailable͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌ ͏‌

The process will consist of two phases:
• <strong>Phase 1</strong>: Technical systems upgrade (estimated up to 2 hours to complete)<br>• <strong>Phase 2<strong>: Trading reboot (estimated up to 2 hours to complete)
During <strong>Phase 1</strong>, our exchange will be put into maintenance mode, and all Coinbase functionality will be disabled across mobile, web and API surfaces. You will not be able to log into your account, trade, or access your funds during this time. Please ensure all transfers are completed ahead of the planned downtime. In addition, we will not modify or cancel any customers orders and any open orders will remain on the order book. <br><br>During <strong>Phase 2</strong>, all functionality will be re-enabled immediately, except for trading. Trading will be re-enabled progressively via our <a ses:no-track href="https://help.coinbase.com/en/exchange/trading-and-funding/what-is-auction-mode-" target="_blank" style="text-decoration: underline; color:#0052FF;"> price discovery auction process</a>. During this process, order books will be placed in auction mode for a minimum of 10 minutes, during which no matches will occur. Customers can post limit orders, cancel outstanding orders, and view the resulting indicative open price. Upon completion of the auction, orders will be matched at the opening price and the order book will be moved to limit-only or full trading mode. <br><br>We know that this might be inconvenient for customers and are working to ensure this needed upgrade happens smoothly.  <br><br>We will be posting reminders and updates on our status page at status.coinbase.com, as well as our X Channel <a ses:no-track href="https://x.com/CoinbaseMarkets" target="_blank" style="text-decoration: underline; color:#0052FF;">@CoinbaseMarkets</a>.
