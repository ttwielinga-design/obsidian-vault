Should I use a protein supplement to hit my daily protein goals?

“Whole food sources (like lean meats, seafood, eggs, dairy,
beans, nuts and tofu) should be the first choice when trying to
hit protein goals, as they have additional nutrients like fiber,
vitamins, and minerals that supplements can't replicate. That
said, if you're consistently falling short of your protein goals
through food alone, a high-quality protein supplement can be a
practical and effective way to bridge the gap. Just think of them
as a supplement to a nutrient dense diet, not a substitute for
one.”

Emily Sullivan, RD

MyFitnessPal Registered Dietitian
