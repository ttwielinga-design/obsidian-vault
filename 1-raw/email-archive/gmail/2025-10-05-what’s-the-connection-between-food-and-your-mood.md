Dietitians’ corner
******************

What is one nutrition habit we could start today to improve our
mood?

"If you're not eating enough fiber, it could be negatively
affecting your mood, so try to incorporate fiber into every meal
and snack--whether it be fruit, vegetables, whole grains, beans
or legumes. Studies show a connection between your gut health and
your mental health, so make your gut happy with prebiotic-rich
fiber-filled foods and a happier mood may follow."

Katherine Basbaum

Registered Dietitian MyFitnessPal
