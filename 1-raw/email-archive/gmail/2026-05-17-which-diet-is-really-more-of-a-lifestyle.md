Is the Mediterranean diet good for your heart health?

“Yes, the Mediterranean diet is widely recognized as
heart-healthy. It emphasizes fruits, vegetables, whole grains,
legumes, fish, olive oil, and nuts—foods that provide healthy
fats, fiber, and antioxidants that help lower LDL cholesterol,
reduce inflammation, and support overall cardiovascular health.
Research consistently shows it’s associated with a lower risk of
heart disease and stroke.” (1
