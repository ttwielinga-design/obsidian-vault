What is your favorite on-the-go high protein snack?

“If I’m out and about, my go-to high protein snack is usually a
whole food bar that I can toss in my bag and have on hand if
hunger strikes. I’m not super picky about brands but I do look
for a couple things on the nutrition label: a hearty dose of
protein (10 grams or more) and a short ingredient list made up of
real, whole foods.”

Katherine Basbaum, MS, RD

MyFitnessPal Registered Dietitian
