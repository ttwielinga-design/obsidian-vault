Dietitians’ corner
******************

How do you balance sweet treats and stay on track with your
health goals?

“When setting health goals, I think a lot about what is
sustainable. I’ve found that enjoying sweets in moderation
actually helps me better achieve my health goals long term.
Knowing that the majority of what I eat throughout the day is
nutrient dense allows me to fully savor and enjoy a sweet treat
from time to time.”

Emily Sullivan

Registered Dietitian
