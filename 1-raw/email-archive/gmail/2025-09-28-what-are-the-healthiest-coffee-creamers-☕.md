Dietitians’ corner

How much coffee creamer should I add to my morning cup of
coffee?

“Morning coffee is a sacred thing that I really don't like to
mess with or give strict recommendations on. So the amount of
creamer you add to your coffee is completely up to you, so long
as you recognize that liquid creamer calories can add up, so try
and measure the amount you add, log it and make sure it fits
within your daily nutrient goals.”

Katherine Basbaum
