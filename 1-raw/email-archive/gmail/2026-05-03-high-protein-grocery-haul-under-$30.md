Dietitians’ corner
******************

How do you put together a balanced meal on a busy weeknight
without breaking the bank?

“For busy nights I look to my freezer stock for a quick meal! I
buy most of my meat at a wholesale store like Costco to save on
cost. For convenience, I then separate raw meats into recipe size
portions such as 1 lb for ground meats or 4-6 chicken filets.
Once I select my meat and set it out to defrost I look for grains
and veggies I have on hand. Getting good at using what you have
on hand can help save you money and also time! A recent example
is lean ground beef and corn tortillas made into smash burgers;
as I had most of the toppings on hand. I also had sweet potatoes
that I cut, seasoned and air fried to make delicious sweet potato
fries. Quick, simple and affordable.”

Brookell White, MS, RD

MyFitnessPal Registered Dietitian
