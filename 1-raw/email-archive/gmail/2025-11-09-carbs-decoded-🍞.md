What is the difference between a refined grain and a whole
grain?

“The difference between a whole grain and a refined grain comes
down to how much of the original, intact grain is present. Whole
grains are the entire grain, which includes the bran, germ and
endosperm. It’s these components that are responsible for the
fiber, healthy fats, vitamins and minerals (2 
( https://link.e.blog.myfitnesspal.com/f/a/oPnbfnb3emLAZqRttOQH-g~~/AAQRxRA~/6wHL_1IeXqz1dKDIpojVfrJu52-yU_Goh67D_VhJZlDorhisDS2UVaNEyzPJP6RC6Urx0C7e-vBSPt3fA5407dq3cVYLikKNJmlGKSIicMZmMgj9abbJaonga24cnnBNpZ1Ob7qEPhLkWCL18u9S_28HfCbmeuutPsawuUYpJdU~ )).”
