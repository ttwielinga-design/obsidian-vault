Dietitians’ corner
******************

What is your #1 weight management tip for enjoying the holiday
season?

“With an abundance of gatherings, treats and holiday meals, it's
easy to throw out healthy habits in the month of December. Try
working towards keeping the habits that have the greatest impact
on your wellbeing and the ones you enjoy most. For me, it's
sticking to my Monday and Friday pilates class and reaching my
daily fiber and protein goals. That way when it comes to a few
days away from the studio, or an extra indulgent meal, I can
fully enjoy it knowing that I’m regularly giving my body what it
needs to feel happy and healthy.”

Brookell White

MyFitnessPal Registered Dietitian
