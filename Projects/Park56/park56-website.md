DESIGN & UI RULES 
 
Visual style: conservative, minimal, classic corporate. Use a restrained palette: deep green 
#0f4d3b (primary), charcoal #222, off-white #f8f7f5, accent muted gold #bfa46f. (If the 
brand already has colors, apply them.) 
 
Typography: Serif-like headline (e.g., Merriweather or Georgia), sans-serif body (e.g., 
Inter/Source Sans). Large headings, generous line-height, clear hierarchy. 
 

Spacing: Generous margins, 120px top hero padding desktop, 48px sections. Mobile: 
compress but keep readable. 
 
Buttons: Primary CTA filled (solid deep green background, white text). Secondary CTA 
outlined. Sticky CTA on scroll (top-right): “Discuss Your Next Move.” 
 
Animations: subtle (fade-up on scroll, 50–200ms) — avoid flashy transitions. No autoplay 
videos or audio. 
 
Accessibility: WCAG AA minimum (contrast ratios, keyboard nav, alt text for all images, skip 
link). 
 
Mobile: Single-column stacking, CTA always visible, hamburger nav.
