**DayVibe Privacy Policy**

**Effective Date:** September 17, 2025  
**Last Updated:** August 15, 2025

Hi there\! Welcome to DayVibe – your voice-first journaling companion that helps you grow through AI-powered insights. We know privacy matters to you (it matters to us too\!), so we've written this policy in simple language to explain exactly how we handle your personal information.

**Who We Are**

DayVibe is developed by \[Company Name\], a company registered in the Netherlands. We're your **data controller**, which means we decide how and why we process your personal information.

**Contact Information:**

* **Company:** \[Company Name\]

* **Address:** \[Address\], Netherlands

* **Email:** privacy@dayvibe.app

* **Phone:** \[Phone Number\]

**What Information We Collect**

**Voice Recordings & Transcripts**

When you journal with DayVibe, we collect:

* Your voice recordings during journaling sessions

* Text transcriptions of your voice recordings

* Timestamps and session metadata

**Emotional & Health Data**

Our AI analyzes your journals to provide:

* Emotional insights and mood patterns

* Goal tracking and progress data

* Personal development recommendations

* Mental wellbeing assessments

**Account & Usage Data**

We also collect:

* Name and email address

* Account preferences and settings

* App usage patterns and analytics

* Device information and technical logs

**Marketing Communications**

If you consent:

* Email preferences and engagement data

* Marketing campaign interactions

**Why We Process Your Data (Legal Basis)**

We process your information based on these legal grounds:

**Explicit Consent (Primary Basis)**

For processing your voice recordings and emotional data, we rely on your **explicit consent** because this information is considered special category personal data under GDPR. You can withdraw this consent anytime.

**Contract Performance**

To provide our core journaling service and fulfill our subscription agreement with you.

**Legitimate Interest**

For app improvements, security, and basic analytics (where it doesn't override your privacy rights).

**How We Use Your Information**

**Core Journaling Features**

* Convert your voice recordings to text

* Generate AI-powered emotional insights

* Track your personal goals and progress

* Provide weekly reviews and recommendations

**Service Improvement**

* Analyze usage patterns to improve our app

* Develop new features and capabilities

* Ensure app security and prevent abuse

**Communications (With Your Consent)**

* Send you insights and weekly reviews

* Share tips for personal development

* Notify you about new features (if you opt-in)

**Freemium vs Premium Processing**

**Free Users Get:**

* Basic voice journaling (up to 2 minutes/day)

* Basic AI summaries (1 per week)

* Basic goal accountability tracking

* Standard data retention (30 days for voice files)

**Premium Users Get:**

* Same as basic users in addiotion to:

* More in-depth summaries

* Clear goal accountability

* Monthly and Quarterly reports

**Important:** We never condition basic journaling functionality on consent to marketing or non-essential processing.

**Who We Share Your Data With**

We work with trusted partners to provide our service:

**AI Processing Partners**

* **OpenAI/Anthropic:** For voice transcription and AI insights

* **Google Cloud/AWS:** For secure data storage and processing

* All partners have signed Data Processing Agreements (DPAs)

**Required by Law**

We may share information if legally required (court orders, regulatory requests, etc.).

**We Never:**

* Sell your personal data to anyone

* Share your voice recordings for advertising

* Use your emotional data for purposes you haven't consented to

**International Data Transfers**

Some of our AI partners are located outside the EU. When we transfer your data internationally, we use:

* **Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)** approved by the EU

* Additional security measures for voice and emotional data

* Transfer impact assessments for high-risk destinations

You have the right to request information about these transfers and safeguards.

**Your Privacy Rights**

You have important rights over your personal data:

**Right to Access**

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you, including voice recordings and AI insights.

**Right to Correction**

Ask us to fix any incorrect or incomplete information.

**Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")**

Request deletion of your personal data. We'll delete:

* Voice recordings immediately

* Transcripts and insights

* All derived data from our systems and partners

**Right to Data Portability**

Export your journaling data in a standard format to use with other services.

**Right to Object**

Opt out of certain types of processing, like marketing communications or analytics.

**Right to Restrict Processing**

Ask us to limit how we use your data while we resolve any concerns.

**Right to Withdraw Consent**

Remove your consent for voice and emotional data processing anytime. This won't affect past processing but will stop future processing.

**How to Exercise Your Rights:**

* Use our in-app Privacy Center

* Email us at privacy@dayvibe.app

* We'll respond within 30 days (usually much faster\!)

**Data Security & Protection**

We protect your information with:

**Technical Measures**

* **AES-256 encryption** for all voice data at rest

* **TLS 1.3 encryption** for data in transit

* Pseudonymization of voice recordings

* Automated security monitoring

**Organizational Measures**

* Role-based access controls (only essential staff)

* Regular security training for our team

* Incident response procedures

* Annual security audits

**Data Retention**

We keep your data only as long as necessary:

**Voice Recordings**

* **Free users:** 30 days maximum

* **Premium users:** Up to 90 days (or longer if you request)

* **User control:** Delete anytime through the app

**Transcripts & Insights**

* Kept as long as you maintain your account

* Automatically deleted when you close your account

* User-controlled retention up to 7 years for goal tracking

**Marketing Data**

* Maximum 3 years from last engagement

* Deleted immediately if you unsubscribe

**Children's Privacy**

DayVibe is not intended for children under 16\. If you're under 16, you'll need verified parental consent to use our service. If we discover we've collected data from a child without proper consent, we'll delete it immediately.

**Cookies & Tracking**

We use essential cookies to make our app work properly. For any non-essential tracking, we'll ask for your consent first. You can manage cookie preferences in our app settings.

**Changes to This Policy**

We may update this policy occasionally. When we do:

* We'll notify you through the app

* We'll explain what's changed

* For significant changes, we may ask for renewed consent

* The updated policy will be posted at dayvibe.app/privacy

**Data Breaches**

If a security incident affects your personal data, we will:

* Notify the Dutch Data Protection Authority within 72 hours

* Inform you directly if there's a high risk to your rights

* Take immediate steps to secure your data

* Provide clear information about what happened and what we're doing

**Marketing Communications**

We'll only send you marketing emails if you consent. You can:

* Opt out anytime using the unsubscribe link

* Manage preferences in your account settings

* Contact us to update your communication preferences

We comply with the EU ePrivacy Directive for all marketing communications.

**Automated Decision-Making**

Our AI provides insights and recommendations about your emotional patterns and goals. These suggestions:

* Are designed to help, not replace human judgment

* Can be turned off in your settings

* Include explanations of how they're generated

* Are subject to human oversight for significant recommendations

You have the right to human review of any AI decisions that significantly affect you.
